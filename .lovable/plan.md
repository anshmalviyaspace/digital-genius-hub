

## Ansh Malviya — Premium Portfolio Website

### Creative Direction
- **Palette**: Deep navy (#0a0a1a) base, indigo (#1e1e5a) surfaces, electric indigo (#4f46e5) accents, with subtle light text (#e2e8f0)
- **Typography**: Syne (bold, expressive headings) + Plus Jakarta Sans (clean body text)
- **Layout**: Broken grid — overlapping elements, off-axis text, asymmetric compositions
- **Animations**: GSAP ScrollTrigger for scroll-based reveals, Framer Motion for hover/entrance animations, smooth parallax layers, text split animations, magnetic cursor effects
